Why choose us?

6 simple steps to use our application!

Step 1

Create an Account

Click on sign up to register an account with us. After registering, proceed to login to complete the other steps.

Step 2

Find a Clinic

Click on 'Make An Appointment' tab to find the clinic around your estate.

Step 3

Make an Appointment

Find your preferred clinic then click on the 'Book' button to make an appointment.

Step 4

Wait for Queue Number

After making an appointment, wait for the clinic to accept your request then, you will be able to see your status changed to "Accepted" and get your queue number.

Step 5

View Booking Status

Click on 'Appointment Status' tab to view your current booking status and queue number.

Step 6

View Visit History

After the visit, you can click on 'Visit History' to see your past appointments.

And tadahh! Enjoy using our application with just 6 simple steps. Thank you!
